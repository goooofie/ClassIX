[DREAMCAST GUIDE]

You do NOT have to install bios to play dreamcast games

REDREAM GUIDE

Open "ClassIX"
Open "roms + emulators + bios"
Open "emulators"
Open "dreamcast"
Run "redream.exe"
Press "Go to library"
Press "Add Directory"
Press "roms + emulators + bios" wherever you have it saved
Press "roms"
Press "dreamcast"
Press your game
Done!
