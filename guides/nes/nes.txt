[NES GUIDE]

You do NOT have to install bios to play NES games

MESEN GUIDE

Open "Classicix"
Open "roms + emulators + bios"
Open "emulators"
Open "nes"
Run "mesen.exe"
In mesen, go to your top left corner
Press "File"
Press "Open"
Go to "roms + emulators + bios"
Open "roms"
Open "nes"
Then open your game
Done!
